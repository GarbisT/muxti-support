Plain Language Summary

MUXTI is a classifieds marketplace for Cyprus. We collect the minimum data needed to run the service. We do not sell your data. We do not store your GPS location. Payments are handled by Stripe — we never see your card number. In-app messages between users are stored on our servers; typing indicators are ephemeral and never stored. 2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Data

When you create a MUXTI account we collect:

  • Email address — used for passwordless OTP authentication and service communications.
  • Display name — shown publicly on your profile and listings.
  • Phone number — optional; shown to buyers only if you enable “Allow Call” on a listing.
  • Profile photo — optional; stored in Supabase Storage (eu-north-1, Stockholm).
  • Expo push token — stored to deliver in-app notifications. Removed when you sign out.

2.2 Listing Data

When you create or save a listing we collect:

  • Title, description, price, category, condition, and listing type (For Sale / Trade / Hire / Free).
  • City name + city-centre coordinates — when you pick a city from the picker, we store the standard centroid latitude and longitude for that city. This is city-level data derived from your explicit selection, not your device’s GPS position.
  • Up to 10 listing photos, stored in Supabase Storage.
  • Contact channel preferences (WhatsApp number, Telegram number, allow-call flag).
  • Listing status, view count, and likes count.
  • Draft status — if you save a listing as a draft before publishing, the draft content (title, description, images, and all other fields you have filled in) is stored on our servers. Draft Listings are visible only to you under “My Listings” and are not published to the public feed until you choose to publish them.
  • Bump record — if you use the one-time Bump option to extend a listing’s active period, we record that the bump has been used (a counter and timestamp). This is used solely to enforce the one-bump-per-listing limit.

2.3 Location Data

Important: what we store vs. what we read

Stored (on listings): City-centre latitude and longitude derived from the city you chose in the picker. Example: selecting “Limassol” stores lat 34.6851, lng 33.0326 — the city centre, not your home.

Read but never stored (for proximity search): If you tap “Use my location” in the Search filters, your device’s GPS coordinates are read once, passed as a query parameter to our database RPC, and immediately discarded. They are never written to any database table or log.

2.4 Content Moderation Data

When you publish or edit a listing, the title and description are analysed for prohibited content. Records of moderation decisions (not the full listing text) are retained in a moderation audit log to comply with the EU Digital Services Act (DSA). Draft Listings are not submitted for moderation until you choose to publish them.

2.5 Payment Data

Payments for Boost plans are processed by Stripe. MUXTI never receives, stores, or has access to your card number, CVV, or bank account details. Stripe is the data controller for payment information. See Stripe’s privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy.

2.6 Usage Data

We collect minimal usage data: listing view counts, likes, and session tokens required for authentication. We do not use third-party analytics SDKs (no Firebase Analytics, no Mixpanel, no Meta Pixel).

2.7 Messaging Data

When you use MUXTI’s in-app messaging feature to communicate with other users about listings, we collect and store:

  • Message content — the text of messages exchanged in a conversation thread, stored on our servers in Supabase (eu-north-1, Stockholm).
  • Message metadata — sender ID, recipient ID, thread ID, and sent timestamp for each message.
  • Read receipt timestamps (read_at) — when you open a conversation, a timestamp is recorded on the messages you have read. This enables the sender to see that their message has been read. You may opt out of read receipts at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security. When either party in a conversation has opted out, no read_at timestamps are recorded or displayed for that conversation.
  • Read receipts preference — your opt-in or opt-out choice for read receipts is stored server-side, linked to your account.

2.8 Real-Time Status Data (Typing Indicators)

Ephemeral — never stored

When you are actively composing a message, a real-time typing signal is broadcast to the other participant in the conversation using Supabase Realtime. This signal contains only a sender identifier and a boolean typing flag. It is never written to any database, is automatically discarded when you stop typing, and is not retained or accessible to us or any third party after transmission.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Authenticate your account via one-time email codes.
  • Save and store Draft Listings on your behalf until you choose to publish or delete them.
  • Display your published listings to other users and power search and proximity filtering.
  • Deliver push notifications about activity on your listings.
  • Facilitate peer-to-peer in-app messaging between buyers and sellers, including delivering messages and, where both parties have opted in, displaying read receipt confirmations.
  • Process Boost payments through Stripe.
  • Automatically review listing content for prohibited material (hate speech, weapons, scams, etc.) as required by EU DSA Article 16 — applied at the point of publication, not while a listing is in Draft status.
  • Enforce the one-time Bump limit per listing.
  • Prevent fraud and abuse, including listing-velocity anomaly detection.
  • Respond to your support requests.

We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal effects (GDPR Article 22).

8. Data Security

  • All data is transmitted over TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Database access is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) policies — users can only read and modify their own data. Draft Listings are accessible only to the Seller who created them. Message threads are accessible only to the two participants in the conversation.
  • Authentication uses short-lived OTP codes and JWT session tokens; no passwords are stored.
  • Supabase infrastructure is hosted in Stockholm (eu-north-1), within the EU.
  • File storage buckets enforce per-user access controls.
  • No employee has direct access to production data without an auditable reason.

Despite these measures, no system is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at boogieapps.help@gmail.com.

10. Local Storage

The MUXTI app stores the following data locally on your device only (not transmitted to our servers):

  • Recent search terms — so your search history is available offline.
  • Filter preferences — so your last-used filters are remembered between sessions.
  • First-listing tips flag — so we only show onboarding tips once.

This local data does not leave your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the app. Note that Draft Listings are stored server-side (not locally), so they are accessible across all your devices and are not affected by uninstalling the app.
