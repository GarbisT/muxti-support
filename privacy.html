Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

MUXTI ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Email address, display name, profile photo (optional)
  • Listing content: Photos, descriptions, prices, and location (city) you provide when posting listings
  • Location: City-level location for listing context; precise GPS may be used during signup (not stored continuously)
  • Messages: In-app chat messages between users
  • Usage data: How you interact with the App (page views, searches, clicks) for improving the experience
  • Device data: Device type and OS version for crash diagnostics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the MUXTI marketplace
  • To process payments via Stripe (we do not store card details)
  • To send transactional emails (OTP codes, listing notifications)
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and prevent fraud

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — our backend infrastructure provider (data hosted in the EU)
  • Stripe — payment processing (card data handled exclusively by Stripe)
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Law enforcement, when required by applicable law

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Deleted accounts and associated data are purged within 30 days. Listing data may be retained in anonymised form for analytics.

5. Your Rights

Under applicable law (including GDPR where applicable), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to certain processing activities
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at support.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), row-level security on our database, and secure authentication. No system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children

MUXTI is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via in-app notification or email.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data requests, contact us at support or boogieapps.help@gmail.com.
