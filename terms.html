44a. Featured Placement Programme

44a.1 — How a Listing Earns Featured Status

The Platform operates an automated Featured Placement Programme that recognises Sellers who make repeated use of the Boost service. Each completed Boost event accrues points to the relevant Listing according to the selected plan:

  • Quick Boost (3 Days) = 2 points
  • Smart Boost (7 Days) = 3 points
  • Max Boost (14 Days) = 6 points

A Listing automatically qualifies for Featured status when it has accumulated a minimum of 6 cumulative Boost points and has completed at least 2 separate Boost events. Points are tied to the individual Listing and do not expire while the Listing remains active on the Platform. No manual claim or additional payment is required to enter the programme.

44a.2 — The Featured Carousel (Display)

Active Featured Listings are displayed in the Featured Carousel — a horizontally scrollable banner positioned at the very top of the main feed, ensuring maximum visibility to every user who opens the Platform. The Featured Carousel is subject to a hard cap of ten (10) active listings at any one time. This cap applies platform-wide, regardless of the total number of qualifying Listings at any given moment.

44a.3 — The Featured Queue (Waiting List)

When a Listing crosses the qualification threshold and all ten carousel slots are already occupied, the Listing is automatically placed in the Featured Queue — a first-in-first-out (FIFO) waiting list ordered by the date and time the Listing qualified. Queue position is permanent once earned: the Listing will not lose its place in the queue simply because a Boost period expires after qualification. The Platform automatically promotes the next eligible Listing from the queue into an open carousel slot approximately every 15 minutes. Sellers can view their current queue position and estimated slot status within the Listing detail screen inside the application.

44a.4 — Carousel Slot Duration and Cooldown

Once a Listing is promoted from the queue into an active carousel slot, it will be displayed in the Featured Carousel for a period of seven (7) consecutive days, after which the slot expires and the next qualifying Listing in the queue is promoted. Following the expiry of a carousel slot, a 30-day cooldown period applies to that Listing before it may re-qualify for a further Featured slot. The cooldown period begins automatically upon slot expiry and requires no action on the part of the Seller.

44a.5 — Queue Integrity and Slot Eligibility

A Listing will be skipped (but not removed from the queue) when a carousel slot becomes available if, at the moment of promotion, the Listing: (a) has been marked as sold or inactive by the Seller; (b) has expired in accordance with clause 20; (c) has been flagged or suspended pending moderation review; or (d) otherwise fails to meet the Platform’s current eligibility criteria. The Listing will retain its queue position and will be considered again when the next slot becomes available and the relevant condition has been resolved. A Listing that is permanently removed from the Platform (e.g., due to a Terms violation) will also be permanently removed from the queue.

44a.6 — Modification Rights

The Operator reserves the right to modify the point values, qualification thresholds, carousel cap, slot duration, cooldown period, and any other criteria applicable to the Featured Placement Programme at any time with reasonable prior notice communicated through the application. Modifications will not retroactively affect Listings that are already active in the carousel at the time the change takes effect.
